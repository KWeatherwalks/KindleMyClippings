- Grab the data from each entry
- Each entry has the following format:
    Line 1: <Title> <(optional parenthetical)> <(author)>
    Line 2: - <Highlight> | <Location> | <Date Added>
    Line 3: <blank>
    Line 4: <Note>
    Line 5: ========== <End of note>

Alternatively, the format for Line 2 is 
- <Highlight> on <Location> | <Date Added>
